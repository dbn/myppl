Anika Schwarzlose was born 1982 in Berlin, Germany. She received a BA from the Gerrit Rietveld Academy in Amsterdam in 2009. At the moment she works in Amsterdam and Sweden, where she follows a MFA program at the Konsthögskolan i Malmö. Her practice revolves around structures, methods and devices we invent to organize the world around us. Often engaging photographic principles like processes of image creation and mediation not only as means but also as subject to her work.
